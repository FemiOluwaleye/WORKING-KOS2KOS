About Us

Who We Are

KOS2KOS, LLC is a locally owned company focused on acquiring and operating small, cash-flowing businesses. We specialize in vending machines and ATM services, with plans to expand into other small business opportunities.

Our goal is simple:
To provide reliable, convenient services while building long-term partnerships with local businesses.

What We Do

1) Vending Machine Services

We install and manage vending machines in businesses and community locations.

We handle everything:

Machine installation

Stocking snacks and drinks

Maintenance and repairs

Product updates based on demand

At no cost to the business.

Warehouses & distribution centers

Offices & commercial buildings

Apartment complexes
